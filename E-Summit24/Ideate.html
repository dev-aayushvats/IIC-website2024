More About the Event

Rules

  • The team size should be 1-3 members.
  • All decisions in matters of eligibility, authenticity & marking made by the judges shall be final and binding.
  • Any attempts at malpractice will lead to disqualification
  • All the event-specific rules and guidelines will be conveyed before the event
  • Organizers reserve the right to change, modify or postpone any aspect of the event without prior notice. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers shall be final and binding.
  • Teams failing to adhere to the deadlines will be disqualified

Rounds

  • The event will take place in two rounds.
  • Participants should register for the event through the registration form. The link for submissions will be shared via email and the participants will be judged based on the below-mentioned criteria. Based on the performance teams will be shortlisted for the final round.

    • Round 1 : Idea Screening Round
    • In this round, all the teams need to describe their start-up ideas through a PowerPoint Presentation which should not be longer than 3 slides (excluding the cover).
    • The presentation should give an abstract idea about the participating team's idea. It will be evaluated by the judges based on the following criteria:
      • The innovation in your idea/Business model
      • The real-world problem it mitigates
      • Market Opportunity for the idea/sustainability
      • The scalability of the idea

    Round 2 : Final Pitching Round
    • 10 teams will be selected for this round.
    • The teams selected for the finals need to pitch their marketing strategy through a presentation to our judges.
    • Each pitch should take no longer than 7 mins (no limit on the number of slides) and would be succeeded by 5 mins of Q&A with the judges.
    • The presentation would be done on Google Meet, the link for the same will be shared via email.
    • The pitch will be evaluated by the judges based on the following criteria (in addition to the judgment criteria of Round 1):
      • The revenue model
      • Feasibility
      • Unique Selling Point

Schedule

  • 17th August 2024 - Registration closes at 11:59 PM as well as the submission portal for presentations for Round 1.
  • 20th August 2024 - Announcement for Round 1 results at 10:00 AM.
  • 24th August 2024 - Round 2 held from 10:45 AM to 12:45 PM (Google Meet).

F.A.Q.

  • How many members can a team have?

    A team must consist of 1 to 4 members.

  • What is a pitch deck competition?

    A pitch deck is a presentation deck that is used to pitch your idea or company to any number of audiences, generally investors. A pitch deck competition is a model version of the real-life counterpart where several teams compete. The audience of the pitch deck in such an event are the judges of the competition.

  • How will the competition be conducted?

    The competition will have two rounds. The first round is an idea screening where the team will be expected to submit a PowerPoint Presentation which should not be longer than 3 slides (excluding the cover), touching on the social impact, innovation, scalability, etc. Then the selected teams will move on to the finals, a presentation round where the teams will present their marketing strategies in front of a panel of judges in about 5 minutes, followed by a Q&A. The final round will be a live round (in a virtual fashion) and will be open for public viewing.

  • Is there a limit for the pitch?

    Yes, the pitch should not cross the time limit of 5 minutes.

  • Will there be prizes?

    Yes, there will be prizes for the winners as well as participation certificates for all the participants.
