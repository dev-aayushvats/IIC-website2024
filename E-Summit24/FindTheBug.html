Live case study competition

About The Event

This inaugural edition consists of a problem statement which is itself unique where the participants are required to find the bugs (or identify the flaws or problems) in the problem statement and prepare a case study for presentation.

Prizes

  • 1st Rs. 6000
  • 2nd Rs. 3000
  • 3rd Rs. 1000
  • Participation certificates for all the participating teams in round 1

When

Saturday
2nd September 2023

More About the Event

Rules

  • The team size should be 2-5 members
  • Being an offline live event, the candidates will be given around 6 hours to complete and the shortlisted candidates will have a 9 minutes presentation and 5 minutes presentation in front of the judges
  • Any attempts at malpractice will lead to disqualification
  • All the event-specific rules and guidelines will be conveyed before the event
  • Organizers reserve the right to change, modify or postpone any aspect of the event without prior notice. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers shall be final and binding
  • Teams failing to adhere to the deadlines will be disqualified

Rounds

  • The event will take place in two rounds.
  • Participants should register for the event through the registration form. The link for submissions will be shared via email. Based on the performance, teams will be shortlisted for the final round.

    • Round 1 : Idea Screening Round
    • In this round, all the teams need to describe their ideas through a PowerPoint Presentation which should not be longer than 5 slides (including the cover).

    Round 2 : Final Pitching Round
    • The teams selected for the finals need to pitch their idea through a presentation.
    • Each pitch should take no longer than 9 mins and would be succeeded by 5 mins of Q&A with the judges
    • The presentation would be done offline, the venue for the same will be shared via email.

Schedule

  • 1st September - Registration closes at 11:59 PM
  • 2nd September - Round 1 will start around 12:30 PM and finishes around 4:15 PM. The round 2 will start as soon as the round 1 is completed.
  • 2nd September - Round 2 held from 4:30 PM to 6:00 PM
  • IIT Dharwad - Location of the Event (Offline)

F.A.Q.

  • How many members can a team have?

    A team must consist of 1 to 5 members.

  • What is the event all about?

    "Find The Bug" is an innovative and engaging event, showcasing a unique challenge where participants are tasked with identifying bugs, flaws, or problems within a specially crafted problem statement.

  • How will the competition be conducted?

    The competition will have two rounds. The first round is an idea screening where the team will be expected to submit a PowerPoint Presentation which should not be longer than 10 slides (including the cover). Then the selected teams will move on to the finals, a presentation round where the teams will present their ideas in front of a panel of judges in about 9 minutes, followed by a Q&A. The final round will be a live round (in a offline fashion) and will be open for public viewing.

  • Is there a limit for the pitch?

    Yes, the pitch should not cross the time limit of 9 minutes.

  • Will there be prizes?

    Yes, there will be prizes for the winners as well as participation certificates for all the participants.
