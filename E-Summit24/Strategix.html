About The Event

For all those cricket maniacs out there. It is a two-round competition, with the first one being a quiz on testing your cricket trivia. For the second round, get a chance to showcase your cricket expertise by strategising the formation of a team that can help your favourite team win the cup.

Prizes

  • First Prize: Rs. 5000
  • Second Prize: Rs. 3000
  • Third Prize: Rs. 2000
  • Participation Certificates for all teams participating.
  • Finalist Certificates for all teams presenting in round two.

When

Round 1:
22rd August

Round 2:
24th and 25th August

Info

More About the Event

Rules and Regulations

  • The team size should be 1-3 members .
  • Any attempts at malpractice will lead to disqualification.
  • All the event-specific rules and guidelines will be conveyed before the event.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change, modify or postpone any aspect of the event without prior notice. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers shall be final and binding.
  • Teams failing to adhere to the deadlines will be disqualified.
  • Shortlisted teams will have to be present offline for Round 2.

Round

The first round (quiz) will be online, hosted on Unstop. 8 shortlisted teams will move on to the second round. The second round is going to be held offline on the campus premises. In the second round, all the members of the registered team are supposed to be present at the mentioned location to participate in the mock auction, using their strategies prepared for the same. The auction is going to take place in two days according to the time mentioned.

Schedule

  • Registrations start on 19th August 2024 and close at 5:00 PM on 23rd August 2024.
  • The first round of quiz is going to be conducted from 7:00 PM to 7:25 PM on 23rd August 2024 on Unstop.
  • The shortlisted teams will be notified about the second round.
  • The second round will take place on 24th (18:00 – 20:00) and 25th August 2024 (16:30 – 18:00)
