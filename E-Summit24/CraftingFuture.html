About The Event

We are excited to present two highly interactive workshops to enhance your skills and broaden your horizons in two critical areas: design thinking and CI/CD pipelines.Each workshop is led by experts with extensive experience, ensuring that participants receive top-tier guidance and hands-on learning. Due to the customized nature of these workshops, seats are limited, and we will close registrations beforehand to ensure a productive experience for everyone involved.

When

25th August, 2024

Info

More About the Event

Workshop 1: Design Thinking and Creativity for Business

  • Led by Bardi Narayana an IIT Madras alumni with a combined experience of 30+ years in entrepreneurship and the field of design.

Workshop 2: CI/CD Pipeline Essentials

  • The workshop is spearheaded by a senior Saksham Chhimwal from our college, who brings industry-level experience from internships at AI-based startups.
  • The session is tailored for those with a basic understanding of software development, version control (such as Git), and command line tools.
  • It will focus on the Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipeline, a crucial aspect of modern software development.

Pre-requisites

  • Basic knowledge of software development, version control, and command line tools.
  • A GitHub account, AWS account, Docker account.
  • Installed GoLang and VS Code.
