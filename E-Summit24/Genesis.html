Genesis: Build your Brand!

Research and Planning Competition

Register

About The Event

The participants are presented with a list of growing market sectors and are tasked with figuring out existing problems or optimisation opportunities which can serve as the foundational stone for a successful and scalable business.

Prizes

  • First Prize: 6000
  • Second Prize: 3000
  • Third Prize: 1500
  • Participation Certificates for all teams participating.
  • Finalist Certificates for all teams presenting in round two.

When

Round 1(online submissions): 15th August, 2024

Round 2: 24th August, 2024

Info

More About the Event

Rules and Regulations

  • The team size should be 1-5 members.
  • Any attempts at malpractice will lead to disqualification.
  • All the event-specific rules and guidelines will be conveyed before the event.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change, modify or postpone any aspect of the event without prior notice. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers shall be final and binding.
  • Teams failing to adhere to the deadlines will be disqualified.
  • Shortlisted teams will have to be present offline for Round 2.

Rounds

  • The first round will be online. Each participating team will have to choose a random market sector from the following list:
      Agriculture
      Renewable Energy Tech
      Smart City Planning
      Environmental Sustainability
      Food Technology
      Tourism and Media
    The team will then have to come up with a proposal and a rough strategy to build a startup in the sector chosen by them and submit a ppt for the same not more than 7 slides (including cover page). 8 Shortlisted teams move to Round 2.
  • The second round is an offline round where all teams get a market sector where they are asked to point out problems and opportunities and then build a brand around it conducting intensive research and propose a detailed business plan. The teams will then present their plan in front of the judges.

Schedule

  • Registrations start on 7th August 2024 and close at 11:59 p.m. on 15th August 2024 along with Round 1 submissions.
  • The shortlisted teams are notified of the updated PS for round 2 on 16th August 2024.
  • The shortlisted teams present their final solutions and plans on 24th August in front of the judges.
