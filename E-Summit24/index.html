About The Event

About The Event

The grand success of the last summit, with over 1500 registrations all across the country, invaluable guests who've stepped forward to taking out their time to enlighten young minds, and enthusiastic students taking part in tough competitions, only left us aiming for a larger audience this time. Thus, we wish to make every entrepreneurial enthusiast have access to this platform and therefore plan on reaching out in all directions.

A bustling crowd of passionate students from premier institutes from across the country, along with riveting quizzes, intriguing pitching competitions, talks from amazing speakers, and much more, is sure to leave anyone with an ecstatic experience to be remembered!

The E-summit 2024 is scheduled to be on 24th August - 25th August, over a span of 2 days full of events, talks, and workshops.

When

Saturday to Sunday
24th August - 25th August

Events

Technostrophe

A thrilling two-round quiz extravaganza spanning diverse topics, culminating in an engaging and dynamic final round!

Ideate

Where innovation meets strategy as participants pitch their startup ideas and marketing strategies in two compelling rounds.

Find The Bug

Given a real-world simulated problem, find the bugs in the current strategies and workflow of a company and provide the best unique solutions.

Intersect

A dynamic panel discussion featuring CEOs and Founders sharing their views and thoughts on various topics relevant in today's scenario.

Finance For All Talk

An event where expert guest speaker(s) unravel the mysteries of finance, offering insights and practical advice to help you navigate and succeed in today's financial landscape.

Genesis

Dive deep into the workings of a market sector, identify hidden opportunities, and build your brand around them!

Crafting the Future

Join expert-led workshops on design thinking and CI/CD pipelines, offering hands-on learning with limited seats—register now!

Strategix

Test your cricket knowledge with a trivia quiz, then strategize the ultimate team to win the cup!

F.A.Q.

  • What is E-summit?

    E-Summit'24 is the entrepreneurial fest hosted by IIC, IIT Dharwad. Featuring well-known personalities, competitions, quizzes, amazing prizes, and much more. E-Summit will provide an insight into many things, empowering students to foster and nurture their creative ideas, innovations, and unique talents.

  • Who all can be a part of the summit?

    Working professionals and students from any college.

  • What are the advantages of attending the summit?

    Apart from the awesome learning experience, you are in for exciting gift cards and certificates from IIC.
