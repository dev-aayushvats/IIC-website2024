About The Event

Are you someone who stays constantly updated with the trends in cryptocurrency, business, startups, entrepreneurship, and technology? Well if yes, then Technostrophe at E-Summit 2022 is the one for you. This time, it is a lot more challenging and fun and is the perfect quiz for you to test your knowledge against others like you. Also, the bright side is that you have the opportunity to win amazing prizes and gain more knowledge.
Don't forget to register since this isn't an opportunity you get every other day!

Prizes

  • Prizes worth Rs.11000
  • Winners will be awarded certificates
  • Certificate of Participation for all participants

When

Saturday to Sunday
20-21 August 2022

Info

More About the Event

Rules and Regulations

  • It would be a group event with a team size of 2
  • Participants in a team can be from 2 different Institutions
  • Only the team leader can take the prelims leg
  • The top 6-7 teams will be shortlisted to battle out in the finals
  • Both the team members can join the call for the finals
  • Rewards and prizes will be announced shortly
  • Technostrophe'22 prelims round is scheduled for 20th August 2022 from 6:30 PM - 7:00 PM
  • There will be 30 questions in total and 30 minutes time for answer them
  • Each correct answer will give you +1 and for all other cases, 0 marks will be given
  • Questions will be in random order for each team
  • You cannot navigate back to a question once you answer it

Schedule

  • Results of the first leg will be announced by 21st August 2022
  • Finals will go on live from 10:30 AM on 21st August 2022.

F.A.Q.

  • What will the questions be about?
    The questions asked will be from the following themes:
    • General Knowledge
    • Entrepreneurship
    • Industrial Touch
    • Business Domain
    • Finance
    • Trending Startups
  • How many members must a team have?

    A team must consist of 2 members.

  • Will there be rounds?

    Yes, there will be 2 rounds, the first will be a prelim. The 6-7 best performing teams will move on to the finals.

  • How will the prelims be conducted?

    The prelims will last for 45 minutes and will be conducted over the Unstop platform. Only one member from the team should give the quiz on the platform, team members may communicate over a call during the quiz.

  • How will the finals be conducted?

    The finals will last for 2 hours and will be held in a live fashion over video call. This round will also be open for public viewing.

  • How can I participate in the quiz?

    You must sign up to participate in the quiz. Hence keep an eye out for the registration links on our social media handles and the E-Summit website!

  • Will there be prizes?

    Yes, there will be prizes for the winners as well as participation certificates for all the participants.
