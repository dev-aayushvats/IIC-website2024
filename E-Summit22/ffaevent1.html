F.A.Q.

  • Why should I attend these workshops?

    The workshop will be covering a lot of the basics of personal finance. Good financial skills can go a long way in helping you make the most out of what you have and help you attain financial stability and independence. Hence the workshops are s must-attend for people at all stages of financial literacy, from those who want to get started with their finances to those who want to brush up on their basics.

    “Finance for All” is a series of 3 workshops on personal finance from 3 of the most renowned financial educators in India. They'll be sharing some of the techniques and strategies they've learned over the years to help you make your money work for you!
